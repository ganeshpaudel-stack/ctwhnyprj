About Chitwan Honey Nepal

Pure Honey Nepal was founded with the goal of promoting natural honey and supporting local beekeepers. We work with farmers from various regions of Nepal, especially from hilly and mountainous areas where bees naturally produce high-quality honey. Our organization focuses on sustainability, fair pricing, and spreading awareness about the health benefits of raw honey. We believe that pure honey should be accessible to everyone and that traditional beekeeping practices must be preserved for future generations.
